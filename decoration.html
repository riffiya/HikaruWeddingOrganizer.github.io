Garden Decoration Wedding

A garden wedding, is perfect for couples who love nature and want an outdoor setting filled with lush greenery and blooming flowers. These weddings are elegant, romantic, and versatile, with a natural ambiance that doesn’t require heavy decorations.

Key Features

Venue: Beautiful gardens, parks, or botanical gardens.
Decor: Floral arches, hanging lights, and natural wooden furniture.
Style: Soft, pastel color palettes with seasonal blooms.
Atmosphere: Fresh, serene, and timeless.

Rustic Decoration Wedding

A rustic wedding, embraces natural textures and countryside charm. It’s perfect for those who love simplicity with a warm, intimate vibe. The focus is on earthy tones and vintage-inspired decor.

Key Features

Venue: Barns, farms, or outdoor fields with a cozy setup.
Decor: Wooden furniture, burlap, mason jars, and fairy lights.
Style: Neutral tones, wildflowers, and handmade touches.
Atmosphere: Intimate, cozy, and heartfelt.

Beach Decoration Wedding

A beach wedding, is ideal for those who dream of exchanging vows with the sound of waves and a stunning ocean view as their backdrop. It’s casual yet captivating, offering a relaxed and romantic vibe.

Key Features

Venue: Private beaches, resorts, or coastal cliffs.
Decor: Driftwood arches, seashell centerpieces, and light fabrics.
Style: Soft blues, whites, and sandy neutrals.
Atmosphere: Relaxed, breezy, and romantic.
