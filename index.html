About

Hikaru Wedding Organizer

Hikaru Wedding Organizer is your trusted partner in creating the wedding of your dreams.
With years of experience, unwavering dedication, and a professional team, we are here to guide you through every step of your journey to your special day.

We understand that every couple has a unique story, and our mission is to ensure that your story is beautifully reflected on your wedding day. From initial planning to the big day, Hikaru is committed to delivering an unforgettable experience.

Wedding Planner

01 - Wedding Preparation

From the moment you begin planning, we’re here to support you every step of the way. We start by understanding your vision, helping you choose the perfect venue, decor, and styling that reflects your personalities and love story. Our team will assist with everything from selecting vendors, finalizing details, to ensuring your schedule is set. Let us handle the stress so you can focus on what truly matters.

02 - The Big Day

On your wedding day, our team will be there to ensure everything runs smoothly. From coordinating the ceremony to managing your guests' needs, we handle the details so you can enjoy the moment. With everything in place, all that’s left is for you to walk down the aisle, ready to begin the rest of your life together.

03 - After the Wedding

Once the celebrations are over, our work continues. We’ll help you with thank-you notes, post-wedding photo sessions, and ensure that every memento from your special day is preserved. We also offer post-event services such as feedback collection to improve our future weddings. Your journey doesn’t end at “I do”—we’re here for all your memories.
