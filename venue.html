Eternal Blossom Garden

  • ✔ Size: 2 hectares

Facilities: Outdoor ceremony area with a floral gazebo. Reception space with a transparent tent and romantic lighting. Stone pathways illuminated by lanterns. Parking space for up to 50 vehicles. Bridal preparation room with full amenities.

Whispering Woods Haven

  • ✔ Location: Yoshino, Nara Prefecture, Japan (known for its ancient cedar forests)

Wooden cabins for bridal preparation and guest accommodation. Rustic-style reception area with wooden tables and cozy seating. Stunning views of Mount Yoshino and surrounding greenery.
